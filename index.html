Guatemala

When I lived in Momostenango, I was exposed to different foods and cultural practices. One of my favorite foods I had while living there was pan mojado (wet bread). The name does not do this delicious dessert justice nor does it give any indication to the flavors that go into creating this delight. Pan mojado is prepared and eaten during holy week (semana Santa). During Semana Santa, you see people of the community stopping by homes of their neighbors, friends and family to give them pan mojado with atol blanco (a hot white cornmeal drink).
